FRIED CHICKEN

Ingredients

  1. 1 lb Chicken, Tenders
  2. 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
  3. 1/4 tsp Onion powder
  4. 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  5. 1/8 tsp Cayenne
  6. 1 1/2 cups Flou
  7. 1 tsp Salt
  8. 1 Oil
  9. 1 Buttermilk

Instructions

  1. To make your own buttermilk chicken tenders, start by soaking the tenders in the buttermilk for at least 30 minutes up to 4 hours.
  2. Next, make the breading by stirring the flour and spices together. The extra crispy, crunchy little bits are made by drizzling a little bit of the buttermilk in the flour and stirring with a fork.
